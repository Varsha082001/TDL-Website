Our Products

TallyPrime

To grow your business, and to make the right business decisions, you need the right insights. To help you in your growth journey, TallyPrime enables you to manage multiple companies and incrementally add features such as multiple go-downs, multi-currency, order process, cost centres etc. This helps you get rid of complexities, and in turn, focus on business growth.

TallyPrime Server

TallyPrime Server offers powerful data server capabilities over the Gold license. This server-based Data Architecture unleashes greater concurrency, secured data access, and advanced monitoring capabilities.TallyPrime Server is truly an Enterprise Class product that helps fast-growing medium and large businesses improve their business efficiencies.

What is TSS?

Tally Software Services (TSS) is a software subscription for a collection of services which add great value to your TallyPrime by giving you the latest developments in technology and statutory laws. Continuous product upgrades, online data exchange, remote access, banking services and much more enhance your business performance by leaps and bounds.

TallyPrime powered by AWS

TallyPrime powered by AWS helps you securely access TallyPrime and your business data anytime, anywhere. It enables multiple users to work on the same data simultaneously while ensuring security and reliability.
