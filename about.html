About Us

Who we are

YENNES TDL, with its Head Office at Chennai, is a specialized provider of Tally Development Language (TDL) solutions. YENNES has expertise in Tally customization, business process automation, integration solutions, and custom report development. We have been designing, implementing, customizing, and supporting TDL solutions for customers across India, helping businesses enhance productivity and streamline operations through tailored TallyPrime solutions.

Explore More

As a GVLA and a 5 Star certified Solutions and Services Partner of Tally, YENNES is best placed to help customers design, implement, integrate and support Tally while providing business specific customization.

Being a long standing partner, the relationship with Tally ensures YENNES access to a much broader range of software tools and solutions for its clients. YENNES, by leveraging on this is able to innovate on the Tally platform developing new skills and capabilities.

While YENNES delights the customer with its domain and technological expertise, the belief is that the real value created to the customer always lies in the relationship.
